Privacy Policy

App: Location SMS Assistant
Effective date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Location SMS Assistant ("the App") handles information.

1. Information We Process

The App may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Phone numbers you enter
  • Message text you compose
  • Location/address details used for emergency SMS content
  • Optional profile and history records

2. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device. The App does not require a cloud account for core features.

3. Data Sharing

The App does not sell your personal information. SMS content is shared only when you choose to send a message through your device's SMS app.

4. Permissions

  • Location: used to include your location in emergency messages.
  • SMS capability: used to open your SMS composer for sending messages.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete message history and local app data at any time from within the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
