Common Issues

Q: SMS cannot be sent.
A: Make sure your device supports SMS and your carrier plan allows text messaging.

Q: Location is unavailable.
A: Enable Location Services for the app in iOS Settings and try again in an open-sky area.

Q: How do I clear my local data?
A: Use the Settings page to clear history and remove saved records.
