Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

App: Location SMS | Effective date: March 2026

1. Information We Process

The app may process phone numbers, message content, location/address details, optional profile fields, and history records.

1. 我们处理的信息

应用可能处理电话号码、短信内容、位置/地址信息、可选个人资料字段和历史记录。

2. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device by default. Core features do not require a cloud account.

2. 数据存储

数据默认存储在您的本地设备中。核心功能不依赖云端账号。

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. SMS content is shared only when you choose to send via your system SMS app.

3. 数据共享

我们不会出售个人信息。短信内容仅会在您主动通过系统短信应用发送时产生共享。

4. Permissions

Location permission is used for adding location details to emergency messages; SMS capability is used to open the system SMS composer.

4. 权限说明

位置权限用于将位置信息加入紧急短信；短信能力用于打开系统短信编辑器。

5. Data Deletion

You can clear history and local app data from within the app settings.

5. 数据删除

您可以在应用设置中清除历史记录和本地数据。
